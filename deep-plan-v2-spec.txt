Deep Plan V2 — Full Day Planning Spec
======================================

## Core Principle
Every day has 24 hours. Plan around FIXED PILLARS, fill remaining time with activities.

## Fixed Pillars (user-adjustable defaults)
1. Sleep: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM (default, user can change)
2. Breakfast: 8:00 AM (at hotel or nearby)
3. Lunch: 12:30 PM
4. Dinner: 7:00 PM
5. Transport: exact times from selected flights/trains
6. Hotel check-in: based on arrival time
7. Hotel check-out: based on departure or standard 11:00 AM

## Day Types (merged — no more separate "travel" vs "explore")

### Arrival Day (e.g., fly Mumbai→Goa, arrive 4 PM)
- 11:47 AM: Leave home
- 12:25 PM: Airport (check-in note)
- 1:55 PM: Flight departs
- 3:00 PM: Arrive Goa Airport
- 4:07 PM: Check into hotel
  → NOTE: "Standard check-in is 3 PM. You're arriving at 4 PM — no early check-in needed."
  → If arriving before 3 PM: "Consider requesting early check-in or leave luggage at reception"
- 4:30 PM: Free time starts
  → Suggest: "You have ~2.5 hours before dinner. Here are nearby activities:"
  → Show 1-2 attractions from user's places or city attractions
- 7:00 PM: Dinner
- 8:30 PM: Evening walk / beach sunset (for beach destinations)
- 10:00 PM: Sleep

### Full Explore Day
- 7:00 AM: Wake up
- 8:00 AM: Breakfast (at hotel or suggest nearby cafe)
- 9:00 AM: Leave hotel
- 9:30-12:00: Morning activities (2-3 attractions)
- 12:30 PM: Lunch (suggest restaurant near last attraction)
- 2:00-5:00 PM: Afternoon activities (2-3 attractions)
- 5:30 PM: Return to hotel / rest
- 7:00 PM: Dinner
- 8:30 PM: Night market / evening walk (optional)
- 10:00 PM: Sleep

### Departure Day (e.g., fly Goa→Barcelona at 6 PM)
- 7:00 AM: Wake up
- 8:00 AM: Breakfast
- 9:00-11:00 AM: Morning exploration (1-2 nearby attractions)
  → "You have the morning free before your flight"
- 11:00 AM: Check out (standard checkout)
  → NOTE: "Standard checkout is 11 AM. Your flight is at 6 PM — request late checkout or store luggage"
- 11:30 AM-2:00 PM: More exploration / lunch
- 2:30 PM: Head to airport
- 3:30 PM: Airport check-in
- 6:00 PM: Flight departs

### Half-Day Transit (arrive morning, depart evening)
- 8:00 AM: Arrive city
- 8:30 AM: Check into hotel (early check-in note)
- 9:00 AM-12:00 PM: Morning activities
- 12:30 PM: Lunch
- 2:00-3:00 PM: Afternoon activity
- 3:30 PM: Check out, head to station
- 5:00 PM: Train departs

## Check-in/Check-out Logic
- Standard check-in: 3:00 PM (editable by user)
- Standard check-out: 11:00 AM (editable by user)
- If arriving BEFORE check-in: show note "Consider requesting early check-in"
- If departing AFTER check-out: show note "Request late checkout or store luggage"
- If check-in and check-out on same day: show note "Day use / no overnight stay"

## Activity Filling Algorithm
1. Calculate free time blocks: gaps between fixed pillars
2. For each free block > 1 hour:
   a. Check user's places for this city
   b. Fall back to CITY_ATTRACTIONS
   c. Distribute activities, ~1.5-2 hours per activity
   d. Add walk/transport between activities
3. Show "Free time" label for remaining gaps
4. Never overlap with meals or transport

## User Controls
- Adjustable wake/sleep time per day
- Adjustable meal times per day (already have start time, extend to all meals)
- Add/remove activities (already implemented)
- Custom activity with time (already implemented)
- Check-in/check-out time override per hotel
- "I want to rest" toggle — removes afternoon activities

About Aiezzy

Aiezzy started as an AI chatbot — and we're grateful for the community that grew around it. But we realized we could solve a much bigger problem.

Every time we planned a trip, we'd end up with 15 browser tabs, a spreadsheet, and still no clear answer to the simplest question: "How much will this trip actually cost, and what does the schedule look like?"

Tools exist that find flights. Others find hotels. Some generate day-level itineraries. But nothing gives you the full picture — total cost from doorstep to doorstep, with a time-based schedule built on real transit data.

That's what we're building. Aiezzy is becoming the AI trip planner that plans your entire journey down to the minute — real prices, real transit times, real schedules — in one place.

We're building this in public. Join the waitlist on our homepage to be the first to try it.
